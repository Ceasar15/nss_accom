About Us

A team of young developers seeking to reduce student's burden on identified campus activities.

We are a team of young developers who seek to eliminate the manual process of complaint submission by students of the University of Ghana-Legon, eliminate the manual process of tracking students currently used by the hall assistants of the same university and also allow students find accommodation easier within areas they have been posted for their national service.

Our Services

Our Main Focus

Digitalized Complaint Submission System

The world as we know today is run by computers, we provide a digital replacement of the manual process by which students submit complaints.

Digitalized Student Tracking System

We cannot digitalize the manual complaint submission process by students and leave the manual means of tracking students on its own can we?

An Accommodation Finder Application

We provide an easy application to allow final year students of the university to find accommodation in areas where they have been posted for national service with ease.
